About Us


We Love Dogs

Like people, dogs need exercise to thrive. Owner and founder, Miriam Gori, has dedicated her life to health and wellness as a personal trainer and massage therapist. Combining her love for animals with a passion for movement has evolved into Windy Bog Doggie PlayCare.

Mom to a high energy boy and two pups, Miriam began hiking to meet thier needs to exercise and explore. She started offering to take the pups of friends and neighbors out on group hikes.
